This website is dedicated to Terry Pratchett, his Discworld works, his fans works, and other related works.
Terry Pratchett lived from April 28th, 1948 to March 12th, 2015 and wrote all 41 books in the Discworld series. The Colour of Magic, the first Discworld novel was published in 1983. The final book in the series, The Shepherd's Crown, was published in August 2015, five months after his death.
